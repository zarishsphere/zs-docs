## 1. Taxonomy Overview

ZARISH-INDEX organises all global standards into **40 primary domains** across **6 meta-layers**. Every entry in the index maps to exactly one domain. Sub-domains provide further classification within each domain.

| Layer | Code | Name | Domains |
|---|---|---|---|
| L1 | Life Sciences | Life Sciences and Health | 1–6 |
| L2 | Physical Sciences | Physical Sciences and Engineering | 7–14 |
| L3 | Society | Society, Governance and Law | 15–22 |
| L4 | Economy | Economy and Trade | 23–27 |
| L5 | Technology | Technology and Infrastructure | 28–35 |
| L6 | Environment | Environment and Natural Resources | 36–40 |

**Domain codes** are two-letter abbreviations used in ZARISH IDs. Example: `HL` for Health and Medical, `HM` for Humanitarian and Emergency Response.

## 2. Meta-Layer 1 — Life Sciences and Health

### Domain 1 — Health and Medical (HL)

The most populated domain in global standards. Covers every formal international standard governing clinical care, public health, epidemiology, medical products, and health systems.

**Key sub-domains:**

| Sub-domain | Key Standards and Bodies |
|---|---|
| Public health and disease surveillance | IHR 2005, EWARS, IDSR, WHO Health Regulations |
| Clinical guidelines and protocols | WHO SMART Guidelines, WHO PEN, mhGAP, IMCIs |
| Pharmaceuticals and good clinical practice | ICH GCP E6(R3), WHO GMP, pharmacopoeias |
| Medical devices and in vitro diagnostics | ISO 13485, IEC 60601, IVD regulations |
| Health informatics and interoperability | HL7 FHIR R5, HL7 v2.x, HL7 CDA, IHE profiles |
| Clinical terminologies and classifications | ICD-11, ICF, ICHI, SNOMED CT, LOINC, MedDRA |
| Laboratory standards | ISO 15189, ISO 17511, ISO 22870 |
| Patient safety | WHO Global Patient Safety Challenges |
| Blood and transplantation | WHO Guidelines on blood, transplantation standards |
| Traditional and complementary medicine | ICTM, WHO TCM Strategy |
| Global health security | IHR 2005, WHO Health Emergency Framework |
| Antimicrobial resistance | WHO GLASS, WHO AWaRe, CAP |
| Mental health | WHO mhGAP, ICD-11 mental/behavioural chapter |
| Non-communicable diseases | WHO PEN protocol, WHO HEARTS |
| Immunisation | WHO SAGE recommendations, EPI standards |

**Primary free sources:** WHO IRIS (OAI-PMH), ISO Open Data (TC 212, TC 210, TC 276), HL7 website, IHTSDO metadata, Regenstrief Institute (LOINC).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 400–600.

---

### Domain 2 — Food Safety and Agriculture (FS)

Standards governing what humans eat and how food is produced. Codex Alimentarius is the dominant body, with ISO TC 34, FAO FAOLEX, and national food safety agencies contributing significantly.

**Key sub-domains:** Food safety management systems (ISO 22000, HACCP principles), Codex commodity standards, pesticide maximum residue limits, food labelling, organic production, GMO/biotechnology standards, aquaculture, food fraud and authenticity, nutritional standards.

**Primary free sources:** Codex Alimentarius website (complete, free), ISO Open Data (TC 34), FAO FAOLEX.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 3,000–4,000 (Codex alone contributes ~3,000 texts).

---

### Domain 3 — Animal Health and Veterinary (AV)

Standards for animal disease control, food-of-animal-origin safety, and animal welfare. WOAH (World Organisation for Animal Health, formerly OIE) is the primary global body.

**Key sub-domains:** WOAH Terrestrial and Aquatic Animal Health Codes (all chapters), zoonotic disease standards, veterinary drug residues, animal welfare during transport and slaughter, aquatic animal diseases, wildlife disease monitoring.

**Primary free sources:** WOAH website (full codes, free), FAO FAOLEX.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–200.

---

### Domain 4 — Plant Health and Phytosanitary (PH)

International standards preventing the spread of plant pests and diseases through international trade. The IPPC (International Plant Protection Convention) is the treaty body; all 46 ISPMs are freely published.

**Key sub-domains:** All ISPMs (International Standards for Phytosanitary Measures) 1–46, pest risk analysis, phytosanitary treatments (heat, fumigation), wood packaging material standards (ISPM 15), plant genetic resources, import/export certification.

**Primary free sources:** IPPC website (complete, free ISPMs at ippc.int), FAO FAOLEX.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 50–100.

---

### Domain 5 — Occupational Health and Safety (OH)

Standards for worker health and safety globally. ILO conventions are the treaty backbone; ISO 45001 is the primary management systems standard.

**Key sub-domains:** ILO OSH Conventions (C155, C161, C170, C176, C184, C187), ISO 45001 management systems, construction safety, mining safety, chemical safety at work, radiation protection for workers, ergonomics, psychosocial risk.

**Primary free sources:** ILO NORMLEX (complete, free), ISO Open Data (TC 283), OSHA publications.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 250–350.

---

### Domain 6 — Pharmaceuticals and Medicines (PM)

Specific standards for pharmaceutical development, manufacturing, and regulation — distinct from Domain 1's broader health systems scope.

**Key sub-domains:** ICH Q series (quality), ICH S series (safety), ICH E series (efficacy), ICH M series (multidisciplinary), GMP standards, pharmacopoeias (US, European, International, Japanese), drug registration dossier requirements, pharmacovigilance, biosimilars.

**Primary free sources:** ICH website (free guidelines), WHO prequalification database, EMA public documents.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 150–250.

## 3. Meta-Layer 2 — Physical Sciences and Engineering

### Domain 7 — Measurement and Metrology (MM)

The foundational science of measurement that underpins every other standard. Without agreed units and calibration, no other standard can be verified.

**Key sub-domains:** SI (International System of Units) — 7 base units and the 2019 redefinition, BIPM publications and CIPM MRA, OIML International Recommendations, national measurement institute standards, calibration and traceability, legal metrology, ILAC MRA for laboratory accreditation, JCTLM reference materials.

**Primary free sources:** BIPM website (free), OIML (free publications), ILAC (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–150.

---

### Domain 8 — Manufacturing and Industry (MI)

Standards for industrial production processes, machinery, and manufactured products across all sectors.

**Key sub-domains:** Industrial automation (IEC 61511, ISO 10218), process safety (IEC 61511), pressure equipment (ASME BPVC), welding (ISO TC 44, IIW), materials testing, machine safety (ISO 12100), industrial gases, textile standards, leather, plastics, rubber, ceramics, packaging.

**Primary free sources:** ISO Open Data (multiple TCs), ASTM metadata (free preview), ASME metadata.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 2,000–3,000.

---

### Domain 9 — Electrical and Electronics (EE)

IEC-domain standards for electrical and electronic devices, safety, and performance. The IEC is the primary body.

**Key sub-domains:** IEC 60364 (electrical installations), IEC 60335 (household appliances), IEC 60601 (medical electrical equipment), semiconductor standards (IEC TC 47), electromagnetic compatibility (IEC 61000), power electronics, batteries and energy storage, lighting standards (CIE, IEC TC 34).

**Primary free sources:** ISO Open Data (IEC via JTC 1 for ICT overlap), IEC website (metadata free via webstore), Wikidata.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 2,000–3,000.

---

### Domain 10 — Construction and Built Environment (CB)

Standards for buildings, infrastructure, and the constructed environment.

**Key sub-domains:** ISO TC 59 building standards, ISO 19650 (BIM/information management), ISO 21930 (environmental product declarations), fire safety (NFPA codes, ISO TC 21), structural engineering codes, building materials (ISO, ASTM, EN), accessibility (ISO/TR 22411), green building (BREEAM references, ISO TC 163 thermal performance).

**Primary free sources:** ISO Open Data (TC 59, TC 268, TC 163), ICC model codes (free summaries), NFPA (free view online).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 500–800.

---

### Domain 11 — Chemical and Process Industries (CP)

Standards for chemical manufacturing, process safety, and hazardous substances management.

**Key sub-domains:** GHS (Globally Harmonised System for Classification and Labelling of Chemicals, UNECE), REACH regulation (EU, internationally referenced), API standards for oil and gas, ISO TC 67 (petroleum, petrochemical), Responsible Care® Global Charter, hazardous substance transport standards.

**Primary free sources:** UNECE GHS Purple Book (free), EUR-Lex (REACH free), ISO Open Data (TC 67), API metadata.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 300–500.

---

### Domain 12 — Materials Science and Testing (MS)

Cross-cutting standards for materials characterisation and testing regardless of end-use application.

**Key sub-domains:** ASTM materials standards (12,000+), ISO materials standards (TC 61 plastics, TC 79 aluminium, TC 17 steel), EN materials norms, corrosion standards, non-destructive testing (ISO TC 135), reference materials (BIPM/IRMM).

**Primary free sources:** ISO Open Data (multiple TCs), ASTM metadata (free preview), Wikidata.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 1,500–2,000.

---

### Domain 13 — Aerospace and Aviation (AA)

Standards governing the design, manufacture, certification, operation, and safety of aircraft and spacecraft.

**Key sub-domains:** ICAO Annexes 1–19 and SARPs, EASA certification specifications (CS-25, CS-23), FAA TSOs, SAE AS9100 (aviation QMS), DO-178C (airborne software), DO-254 (hardware assurance), RTCA standards, military aviation standards (MIL-STD-461 EMC, MIL-STD-810 environmental).

**Primary free sources:** ISO Open Data (TC 20), ICAO summaries and Annex TOC (free), SAE metadata.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 400–600.

---

### Domain 14 — Space and Satellite (SS)

Standards for space systems, spacecraft data communications, and satellite operations.

**Key sub-domains:** CCSDS Blue Books (all 200+ freely downloadable from public.ccsds.org), ECSS (European Cooperation for Space Standardisation, free from ecss.nl), ISO TC 20 SC 14, ITU Radio Regulations for satellite orbital slots, COPUOS guidelines for space debris mitigation, emerging commercial space standards.

**Primary free sources:** CCSDS (all free), ECSS (all free), ISO Open Data (TC 20 SC 14), ITU Radio Regulations (summary free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 150–250.

## 4. Meta-Layer 3 — Society, Governance and Law

### Domain 15 — Human Rights (HR)

The full architecture of international human rights law and standards.

**Key sub-domains:** International Bill of Human Rights (UDHR, ICCPR, ICESCR), the nine core UN human rights treaties (CEDAW, CRC, CRPD, CAT, CMW, CERD, CED), General Comments and Recommendations of treaty bodies, UN Human Rights Council resolutions, Special Procedure mandates, UNGPs on Business and Human Rights, regional human rights instruments (ECHR, ACHPR, ACHR, Arab Charter).

**Primary free sources:** OHCHR (complete, free), UN Treaty Collection, Wikidata.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–200.

---

### Domain 16 — Labour and Employment (LE)

ILO and related standards governing work, employment, and labour relations globally.

**Key sub-domains:** All 190 ILO Conventions and 206 Recommendations, 1998 Declaration on Fundamental Principles and Rights at Work, Maritime Labour Convention 2006, social protection floors, employment policy, labour administration and inspection, OECD Guidelines for MNEs on employment.

**Primary free sources:** ILO NORMLEX (complete, free, with API), ILO website.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 400–450. *Note: ILO NORMLEX bulk ingestor already produces 252+ entries.*

---

### Domain 17 — Humanitarian and Emergency Response (HM)

Standards governing humanitarian action, emergency response, and protection in crises. Directly relevant to CPI Bangladesh operations in Cox's Bazar.

**Key sub-domains:** Sphere Handbook 2018 (WASH, food, shelter, health minimum standards), Core Humanitarian Standard (CHS) — 9 commitments, INEE Minimum Standards for Education in Emergencies, IASC Cluster System and guidelines, UNHCR policies (GBV, RSD, AGD, Solutions), ICRC Customary IHL, Code of Conduct for the IRCR and NGOs, WHO Emergency Medical Teams (EMT) standards, Mass Casualty Management, Camp Coordination and Camp Management (CCCM), Minimum Economic Recovery Standards (MERS), CALP Network standards.

**Primary free sources:** Sphere (free), CHS Alliance (free), INEE (free), IASC (free), UNHCR refworld (largely free), Reliefweb.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 150–250.

---

### Domain 18 — Legal and Commercial Law (LC)

International legal standards, model laws, and commercial law frameworks.

**Key sub-domains:** UNCITRAL Model Laws (arbitration, e-commerce, insolvency, procurement), CISG (UN Convention on Contracts for the International Sale of Goods), UNIDROIT Principles, ICC Incoterms® 2020, ICC UCP 600, New York Convention on arbitral awards, Rome Statute of the ICC, Palermo Protocols.

**Primary free sources:** UNCITRAL (all free, uncitral.un.org), UNIDROIT (all free, unidroit.org), ICC summaries (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–200.

---

### Domain 19 — Governance, Transparency and Anti-Corruption (GT)

Standards for good governance, institutional accountability, and anti-corruption.

**Key sub-domains:** UNCAC (UN Convention Against Corruption), OECD Anti-Bribery Convention, ISO 37001 (anti-bribery management systems), ISO 37301 (compliance management), ISO 37000 (governance of organisations), EITI Standard 2023, Open Government Partnership commitments, GRECO standards.

**Primary free sources:** UNODC (UNCAC free), OECD (free), ISO Open Data (TC 309), EITI (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–120.

---

### Domain 20 — Education and Research (ER)

Standards for educational systems, institutions, qualifications, and research practices.

**Key sub-domains:** UNESCO Conventions on recognition of qualifications (Lisbon, Tokyo, Addis), ISO 21001 (educational organisations management), ISCED (International Standard Classification of Education 2011), Declaration of Helsinki (medical research ethics), FAIR Data Principles, ORCID/DOI/ISBN/ISSN as research identifier standards, UNESCO Science Report frameworks, OECD PISA methodology.

**Primary free sources:** UNESCO (free), ISO Open Data (TC 232), OECD (largely free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–120.

---

### Domain 21 — Culture, Heritage and Arts (CH)

Standards protecting and promoting cultural heritage, the arts, and intangible heritage.

**Key sub-domains:** UNESCO World Heritage Convention 1972, UNESCO Convention for the Safeguarding of Intangible Cultural Heritage 2003, UNESCO Convention on Diversity of Cultural Expressions 2005, 1954 Hague Convention for Protection of Cultural Property in Armed Conflict, ICOM Code of Ethics for Museums 2022, ICOMOS International Charters (Venice Charter, Burra Charter, Nara Document), ICCROM conservation guidelines.

**Primary free sources:** UNESCO (free), ICOMOS (all charters free, icomos.org), ICOM (free), ICCROM (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 60–100.

---

### Domain 22 — Sports and Recreation (SR)

International standards for sports governance, anti-doping, and physical recreation.

**Key sub-domains:** WADA Code 2021 and International Standards (Testing, Laboratories, TUE, Results Management), UNESCO Convention Against Doping 2005, IOC Olympic Charter, FIFA Regulations (Laws of the Game, Statutes), World Athletics Technical Rules, World Aquatics Rules, ISO TC 83 (sports and recreational equipment), CAS Arbitration Code.

**Primary free sources:** WADA (free), UNESCO (free), IOC (free), FIFA (free), World Athletics (free), CAS (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 60–100.

## 5. Meta-Layer 4 — Economy and Trade

### Domain 23 — Finance, Banking and Accounting (FB)

International standards governing the global financial system.

**Key sub-domains:** Basel I/II/III/IV (BCBS capital and liquidity), IOSCO Principles of Securities Regulation, IAIS Insurance Core Principles, FATF 40 Recommendations on AML/CFT, FSB Key Standards, IFRS (all 17 standards), IAS (all active), ISSB S1/S2, ISAs (auditing), IESBA Code of Ethics, CPMI Principles for Financial Market Infrastructures, IADI Core Principles for deposit insurance, IMF SDDS/e-GDDS, ISO 20022 (financial messaging).

**Primary free sources:** BIS/BCBS (free), FSB (free), IOSCO (free), FATF (free), IFRS Foundation (free account), IAASB/IFAC (free), IMF (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 300–500.

---

### Domain 24 — Trade and Customs (TC)

Standards governing international trade facilitation, customs procedures, and market access.

**Key sub-domains:** WTO agreements (GATT, TRIPS, SPS, TBT, GATS, Trade Facilitation Agreement), WCO Harmonized System (HS 2022), UN/CEFACT trade facilitation recommendations, UNCTAD ASYCUDA, AEO (Authorised Economic Operator) standards.

**Primary free sources:** WTO (all legal texts free), WCO HS (chapter metadata free), UN/CEFACT (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–200.

---

### Domain 25 — Supply Chain and Logistics (SL)

Standards for end-to-end supply chain operations, product identification, and traceability.

**Key sub-domains:** GS1 global standards (GTIN, GLN, SSCC product identifiers), EPCIS track-and-trace, GS1 GTS2, WHO prequalification supply chain standards, Sphere logistics minimum standards, cold chain and temperature standards (WHO PQS), ISO TC 122 (packaging).

**Primary free sources:** GS1 website (metadata free), ISO Open Data (TC 122), WHO (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–120.

---

### Domain 26 — Sustainability, ESG and Circular Economy (SE)

Standards for environmental, social, and governance reporting, and sustainable business practices.

**Key sub-domains:** GRI Standards (Universal Standards GRI 1/2/3 + Sector Standards), SASB Standards (77 industry standards), ISSB IFRS S1/S2, EU ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards, mandatory via CSRD), GHG Protocol, TCFD recommendations, CDP disclosure framework, ISO 14001 (environmental management), ISO 14064 (GHG quantification), ISO 20121 (sustainable events).

**Primary free sources:** GRI (free), SASB (free), IFRS Foundation (free account for ISSB), EU ESRS via EUR-Lex (free), GHG Protocol (free), TCFD (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 150–250.

---

### Domain 27 — Taxation and Public Finance (TP)

International standards governing tax policy, public financial management, and fiscal transparency.

**Key sub-domains:** OECD BEPS (15 Actions, Pillar One/Two), Common Reporting Standard (CRS), OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines, OECD Fiscal Transparency standards, IMF GFS Manual, IPSAS (International Public Sector Accounting Standards), INTOSAI standards for supreme audit institutions.

**Primary free sources:** OECD (largely free), IMF (free), IPSASB/IFAC (free), INTOSAI (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 50–80.

## 6. Meta-Layer 5 — Technology and Infrastructure

### Domain 28 — Information and Communications Technology (ICT)

The broadest technology domain. IETF, ISO/IEC JTC 1, W3C, IEEE, and ITU-T collectively govern the internet and digital infrastructure.

**Key sub-domains:** IETF RFCs (9,400+ total — the technical specifications of the internet), W3C Recommendations (HTML, CSS, WebAuthn, WCAG, RDF), ISO/IEC JTC 1 standards (metadata from ISO Open Data), IEEE 802 standards (Wi-Fi, Ethernet), OASIS standards, ECMA standards (all free), ETSI standards catalogue, ITU-T Recommendations.

**Primary free sources:** IETF RFC Editor (all free, rfc-editor.org), W3C TR index (all free), ISO Open Data (JTC 1), ECMA International (all free), ETSI (catalogue free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 12,000–15,000. *Note: IETF alone contributes 9,400+ entries.*

---

### Domain 29 — Cybersecurity and Data Privacy (CY)

Standards for information security, cybersecurity management, and data protection.

**Key sub-domains:** ISO/IEC 27001 family (information security management), ISO/IEC 27005 (risk management), NIST SP 800 series (complete — all free), NIST FIPS (Federal Information Processing Standards), NIST Cybersecurity Framework, IEC 62443 (industrial cybersecurity), GDPR and major national privacy regulations (CCPA, PIPL, PDPA, LGPD, POPIA), ENISA guidelines, PCI DSS.

**Primary free sources:** NIST CSRC (all free), ISO Open Data (27000 series metadata), ENISA (free), EUR-Lex (GDPR free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 300–400.

---

### Domain 30 — Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies (AI)

Standards for AI governance, ethics, and emerging technologies including quantum computing.

**Key sub-domains:** ISO/IEC 42001:2023 (AI management systems), ISO/IEC JTC 1 SC 42 output, ISO/IEC JTC 3 (Quantum Technologies, new 2024), NIST AI RMF 1.0 (free), OECD AI Principles (adopted by 42+ countries), UNESCO Recommendation on AI Ethics 2021, EU AI Act 2024 (full text free), IEEE 7000 series (ethics in AI), ITU AI Standards.

**Primary free sources:** NIST (free), OECD (free), UNESCO (free), EUR-Lex EU AI Act (free), ISO Open Data (JTC 1 SC 42 metadata).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–150.

---

### Domain 31 — Energy and Utilities (EU)

Standards governing energy production, distribution, and the transition to renewable systems.

**Key sub-domains:** IAEA Safety Standards (all 250+ documents free — GSR, SSR, GSG, SSG series), ISO 50001 (energy management), IEC TC 82 (photovoltaics), IEC TC 88 (wind turbines), IEEE 2030 (smart grid), ITU-T recommendations for smart energy grids, ISO 55001 (asset management for utilities).

**Primary free sources:** IAEA (all free, iaea.org), ISO Open Data (TC 301), IEC metadata (webstore), IEEE (summary free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 400–600.

---

### Domain 32 — Transport (TR)

Standards governing road, rail, aviation, maritime, and multimodal transport.

**Key sub-domains:** ICAO Annexes 1–19 and SARPs (structure free; full text subscription), IMO Conventions (SOLAS, MARPOL, STCW, MLC 2006, ISPS, Polar Code), UNECE ADR (road transport of dangerous goods), UNECE RID (rail), UN Model Regulations, ISO TC 22 (road vehicles), ISO TC 8 (ships and marine technology).

**Primary free sources:** ICAO (Annex structure free), IMO (convention summaries free), UNECE (ADR/RID free), ISO Open Data (TC 22, TC 8).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 500–800.

---

### Domain 33 — Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WS)

Standards for safe water, sanitation systems, and hygiene promotion. Critical for humanitarian operations.

**Key sub-domains:** Sphere WASH minimum standards, WHO Guidelines for Drinking-Water Quality (GDWQ), WHO/UNICEF JMP methodology and service ladders, ISO TC 224 (drinking water and wastewater services), WPDx (Water Point Data Exchange), UNHCR WASH technical standards.

**Primary free sources:** Sphere (free), WHO (GDWQ free), JMP (free), ISO Open Data (TC 224).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 60–100.

---

### Domain 34 — Built Environment and Urban Systems (BU)

Standards for sustainable cities, smart urban systems, and urban planning frameworks.

**Key sub-domains:** ISO 37100–37123 series (sustainable communities and cities), IEC SyC Smart Cities, ITU-T Y.4000 series (IoT and smart cities), ISO 37120 (indicators for city services), ISO 37122 (smart city indicators), ISO 37123 (resilient cities).

**Primary free sources:** ISO Open Data (TC 268), IEC metadata (webstore), ITU-T (recommendations index free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–150.

---

### Domain 35 — Defence and Security (DS)

Declassified subset of defence and international security standards, plus arms control treaties.

**Key sub-domains:** UN Arms Trade Treaty (ATT), Ottawa Anti-Personnel Mines Treaty, Convention on Cluster Munitions (CCM), Chemical Weapons Convention (CWC), Biological Weapons Convention (BWC), Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty (NPT), CTBT, Wassenaar Arrangement (public control list), IAEA safeguards system, NATO basic documents (free from nato.int), US MIL-STD-461G and MIL-STD-810H (free from quicksearch.dla.mil).

**Primary free sources:** UN Treaty Collection (free), IAEA (free), MIL-STD downloads (free from DLA), NATO (basic documents free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–120.

## 7. Meta-Layer 6 — Environment and Natural Resources

### Domain 36 — Environment and Climate (EC)

Standards governing environmental protection and the international response to climate change.

**Key sub-domains:** UNFCCC and Paris Agreement framework, CBD (Convention on Biological Diversity), Kyoto Protocol, Ramsar Convention (wetlands), Stockholm Convention (persistent organic pollutants), Basel Convention (hazardous waste), Rotterdam Convention (prior informed consent), Minamata Convention (mercury), ISO 14001 (environmental management), ISO 14064 (GHG quantification), GHG Protocol standards.

**Primary free sources:** UNFCCC (free), UNEP (all conventions free), CBD (free), ISO Open Data (TC 207), GHG Protocol (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 200–300.

---

### Domain 37 — Marine and Ocean (MO)

Standards governing ocean use, maritime operations, and marine environmental protection.

**Key sub-domains:** UNCLOS (560 articles — the constitution of the oceans), IMO Conventions (SOLAS, MARPOL, STCW, ISM Code, ISPS Code, Ballast Water Convention, Polar Code), FAO Code of Conduct for Responsible Fisheries, CCAMLR Conservation Measures, Regional Seas conventions (Barcelona Protocol, Cartagena Convention, OSPAR, Nairobi Convention).

**Primary free sources:** UN Treaty Collection (UNCLOS free), IMO (convention summaries free), FAO (code of conduct free), UNEP Regional Seas (free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 100–150.

---

### Domain 38 — Biodiversity and Conservation (BC)

Standards for protecting biodiversity, managing wildlife trade, and conservation practice.

**Key sub-domains:** CBD and Kunming-Montreal Global Biodiversity Framework, Nagoya Protocol (access and benefit sharing), CITES (Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species) — all three Appendices, Ramsar Convention, IUCN Red List criteria and categories, IUCN Protected Area Management Categories.

**Primary free sources:** CBD (free), CITES (free), IUCN (criteria free), Ramsar (free), UN Treaty Collection.

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–120.

---

### Domain 39 — Disaster Risk and Preparedness (DR)

Standards for disaster risk reduction, emergency preparedness, and community resilience.

**Key sub-domains:** Sendai Framework for Disaster Risk Reduction 2015–2030 (7 global targets, 38 indicators), ISO 22301 (business continuity management), ISO 22320 (emergency management), ISO TC 292 full suite, UNDRR Terminology on Disaster Risk Reduction, Sphere Handbook (overlaps with Domain 17 — cross-referenced).

**Primary free sources:** UNDRR (Sendai free), ISO Open Data (TC 292), UN Office (Sendai framework free).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 50–80.

---

### Domain 40 — Extractive Industries and Natural Resources (EX)

Standards for responsible mining, oil and gas, forestry, and natural resource management.

**Key sub-domains:** EITI Standard 2023 (Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative — full text free), ICMM 10 Principles for Sustainable Mining, Kimberly Process Certification Scheme (conflict diamonds), RSPO Principles and Criteria (sustainable palm oil), Bonsucro Standard (sugarcane), FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) — chain of custody, PEFC standards, API standards for oil and gas (metadata free).

**Primary free sources:** EITI (free, eiti.org), ICMM (free), CITES (Kimberly context), RSPO (free summaries), ISO Open Data (TC 82 — petroleum).

**Estimated entries (v1.0):** 80–120.
